Growing up overweight has affected my mentality and how I perceived myself when out in public: often awkward and embarrassed. It was not until I started to exercise did the weight scale go down and I became confident. Although now I have not followed my exercise routine and fell back into bad habits. Therefore, this will be a page dedicated to learning more about being healthy and the different types of food I could consume to help me get back on track. I often use Quora, Reddit, and Youtube to find help on developing good eating habits.

    Foods to Go Cold Turkey On:


  • Sugary drinks: pop, juice, powdered mixtures, etc
  • Processed snacks: chips, crackers, cookies, granola, etc
  • Candy and unhealthy fats: Hersey's, beef, fried food, etc

      • Foods to Look More Into:


  • Veggies: fresh, frozen, canned
  • Fruits, but preferably whole fruits
  • High protein/fiber: eggs, cheese, lean cuts of meat, etc
